People and Blogs is a weekly newsletter, delivered every Friday, where I ask interesting people to talk about themselves and their blogs.

The goal is to both highlight wonderful human beings and their blogs, and also to promote a healthier way to inhabit the web and show that traditional social media is not the be all and end all when it comes to having an internet presence. Learn more about P&B
